🏃 Why Being Active Matters

Regular exercise helps children stay strong, energetic, and focused. Spending at least one hour being active each day can improve both physical and mental health.

Activities such as cycling, running, skipping, and playing outdoor games are great ways to stay fit and have fun.

🍎 Healthy Lifestyle Tips

  • 🥗 Eat a balanced diet every day
  • 💧 Drink plenty of water
  • 😴 Get enough sleep each night
  • 🚴 Stay physically active
