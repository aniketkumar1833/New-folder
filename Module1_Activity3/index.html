🌟 Voices of Great Minds

Throughout history, inspiring people have shared ideas that continue to motivate millions. Let's explore some memorable quotes.

Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs famously said: Stay hungry, stay foolish.

The only way to do great work is to love what you do.

Mahatma Gandhi

Gandhi encouraged people by saying: Be the change that you wish to see in the world.

The future depends on what you do today.

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. Kalam inspired students with: Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.

Excellence is a continuous process and not an accident.

Walt Disney

Walt Disney believed that All our dreams can come true, if we have the courage to pursue them.

The way to get started is to quit talking and begin doing.
